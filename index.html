About Me

I'm a Software Engineer specializing in DevOps.

In my career I have developed systems, processes and builds for multi OS and multi platform development, Web applications and Cloud infrastructure, System Administration (Linux & Windows), source control ( Git!) and implemented ALM tools and methodologies.
I tend to feel at home in cross product teams whose responsibilities include multiple technologies, software stacks and architectures.

I started my career at a multinational software provider which was a major learning experience from so many talented professionals in diverse fields.
Currently, I'm focusing on taking my experience to companies where I can use cutting edge technologies and make more of an impact.

I'm passionate and curious about everything technology and enjoy listening to podcasts regularly to expand my knowledge, among them:

  • Software Engineering Daily (On interesting new and emerging software technologies)
  • Masters Of Scale (Reid Hoffman on building tech companies and entrepreneurship)
  • Hillel Fuld (On startups and the tech scene in Israel)
I especially enjoy tackling new technical challenges and bringing the perfect solution for the problem, and I'm never shy of the cutting edge..
